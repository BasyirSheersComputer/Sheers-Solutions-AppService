About

A warm intro on who we are.

  • 2018-2021

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our journey began in a small garage with a big dream. Fueled by passion and dedication, we started as a group of friends eager to make a difference in the tech world. Late nights and countless cups of coffee marked our early days, as we brainstormed ideas and worked tirelessly to bring them to life.

  • March 2021

    Our Company is Born

    Our vision took a formal shape as we officially launched our company. It was a monumental step forward, transitioning from a hobby project to a business entity. We were excited to introduce our innovative solutions to the market and were overwhelmed by the positive reception from our early customers.

  • December 2021

    Transition to Full Service

    We had evolved significantly. Our services expanded, and our team grew. This transition was marked by the introduction of comprehensive solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. We moved into a larger office space, better equipped to handle the increasing demands of our growing client base.

  • July 2022

    Phase Two Expansion

    We embarked on Phase Two of our expansion. This phase was characterized by strategic partnerships and entry into new markets. We launched new product lines and enhanced our service offerings, ensuring that we stayed ahead of the curve in an ever-evolving industry.
